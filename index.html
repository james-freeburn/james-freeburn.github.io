Introduction

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the University of Sydney, where I study fast optical transients with a particular focus on afterglows and kilonovae. My work is observationally driven, aiming to characterise these explosive events and how they evolve across wavelengths. My current research involves developing strategies to discover and characterise these transients with the Vera C. Rubin Observatory.
